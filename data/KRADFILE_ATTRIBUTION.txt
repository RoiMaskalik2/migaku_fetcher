Format: a flat JSON object mapping each kanji to the list of component
radicals/primitives it decomposes into, e.g. {"蝙": ["｜","一","戸","虫","冊","尸"]}.
Source: kradfile-u (the expanded Unicode edition of KRADFILE, covering
JIS X 0208/0212/0213 kanji).
